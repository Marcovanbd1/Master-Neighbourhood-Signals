Commissioning Neighbourhood Signals

Neighbourhood Signals is commissioned as civic infrastructure, not as software.

The 30-day sprint produces the evidence. Commissioning is the response.

What changes locally

Neighbourhood Signals becomes part of how the place listens, plans, and responds, not a time-limited project.

The commissioning tiers

We’re listening. We’re responding. We’re embedded. Anchor neighbourhood.

The delivery partner
